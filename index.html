Let's Make Earth Green Again

The environment is our home. Every small action we take today can create a better future for tomorrow.

About Environment

The environment includes all living and non-living things around us. Clean air, fresh water, forests, and wildlife are essential for life. Human activities such as pollution and deforestation are harming nature.

Major Environmental Problems

Air Pollution

Smoke from vehicles and factories pollutes the air and causes diseases.

Deforestation

Cutting trees destroys habitats and increases global warming.

Water Pollution

Waste dumped into rivers and lakes harms aquatic life.

How Can We Help?

  • 🌱 Plant more trees
  • ♻ Reduce, Reuse and Recycle
  • 🚲 Use bicycles or public transport
  • 💧 Save water
  • ⚡ Save electricity
  • 🚯 Avoid littering
